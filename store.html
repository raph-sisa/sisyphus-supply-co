Editor's Pick

The Meanwhile Clock

A machined aluminum desk clock with no alarm, no date, and no countdown. It tells the current time only. It doesn't track how much time is left, because nobody knows that, and pretending otherwise was making everyone anxious.

The second hand moves at exactly one second per second, which we found, after extensive testing, to be the correct speed. When the battery dies, you replace it. The clock keeps going. We liked the metaphor enough to ship it.
